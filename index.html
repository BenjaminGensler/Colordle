How To Play

- Click on the colors to change the color

- Create a color combination and press "Guess"

- Your guess will tell you about yoru combination:

- you have the right color in the right spot

- you have the right color in the wrong spot

- you have the wrong color


- Keep guessing until you get the right combination

**Note:
There can be duplicate colors. If there is a duplicate color the guess will not include it in the results.
